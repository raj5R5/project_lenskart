About Lenskart

Lenskart is an online eyewear store that offers a wide selection of prescription glasses, sunglasses, and contact lenses at affordable prices. Our mission is to provide quality eyewear to customers all over the world while offering exceptional customer service and support.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe in providing our customers with the highest quality eyewear available.
  • Affordability: We strive to offer our customers the best prices possible without compromising on quality.
  • Customer Service: We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and support to all of our customers.
  • Innovation: We are constantly exploring new ways to improve our products and services to better meet the needs of our customers.

Our Team

We have a dedicated team of eyewear experts who are passionate about helping our customers find the perfect pair of glasses. From our customer service representatives to our optometrists, we are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.
